About


Blackhat Dealership opened its door in the spring of 1998. James Blackhat decided he wanted to give the buyers more of a selection and affordability.
What made the dealership different from the competitors was that Mr. Blackhat cared about the family that was buying the vehicle.
Blackhat offered free lifetime oil changes on the vehicles to assure it ran smoothly and never had any problems.
If your vehicle did break down you had free road size assistance.
Not only is this one of the most trusted dealerships in the area, but when your buying a car you're also entering a family.
